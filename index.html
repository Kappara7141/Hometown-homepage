Visit Torshov

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant
district close to the center of Oslo.

Top three activities to do at Torshov

Attend a show or a concert

The torshav theatre is a great place to spend your night out.

Go for a picnic in the local park

The torshav park is perfect for picnic, getting some tan or just chilling out.

Go sweaty at the sports field

Play volley ball, foot ball, basket ball or lift some weights.
